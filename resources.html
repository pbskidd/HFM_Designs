Resources

HfM Design Consulting can provide all levels of supplier management, from total management solutions down to integration with your current relationships.

When you want professional results, you need to trust the experts. Not every supplier will provide you the level of sophistication your product requires. From the most glamorous manufacturing outfit to the garage shop, all have their purpose for you to benefit from.

I have worked with many different suppliers over the years, and I have built strong and trust worthy relationships. Let me pull some strings for you!

I can help you achieve results and work with any of the specialties below, and more.

3D Printng

This is probably the biggest manufacturing hype of the century. With a large variety of materials to choose from, there is most certainly one close enough to mimic your preliminary ideas. Also often used for check the ergonomic of a product, this manufacturing method is often used as a first step.

CNC Rapid Prototyping

HfM Design Consulting can assist you in the CNC manufacturing of your designs. The files are sent to a CNC rapid prototyper and the finished parts are returned to you within a few days at a very competitive cost.

This is a great option to save time and money for this phase of manufacturing. The parts will then be transferred to a trusted model maker for completion.

Model Making

While your design is going through the iteration process, the clock does not stop. The faster you can get all your parts, and test them, the better. The model maker, in collaboration with the designer will enhance the design to ensure functionality and, if necessary, redline the preliminary specifications.

Injection Molding

For some applications it is necessary to mold components in order to evaluate their functionality. HfM design consulting can source test parts and soft tooling, even short production runs within a few days, to get you to market fast.

Packaging

HfM Design Consulting can help you with your packaging needs. On first production runs, the focus will be on low cost and flexibility. This process generally takes from one to three weeks.

On large production runs, other solutions are available to maximize upon economies of scale.

Production Mfg.

Working with the right supplier is critical. You need your product to meet specification, and on-time delivery. Various factors to consider when choosing the right supplier: size, capabilities, capacity, to meet your growing demand?

HfM Design Consulting can help you evaluate the right supplier for your product, with a vast network of certified suppliers from cost to coast.

Testing

The testing stage is the culmination of all the stages preceding it.

You require a certified testing partner who is also qualified to execute your specific testing needs. Whether mechanical bench testing, biomechanical testing, or laboratory testing, you need responsive, expert service.

Regulatory Submission

HfM Design Consulting will align you with resources for all your regulatory needs. Expert execution on notifications to the FDA and other regulatory agencies can save months of time and substantial amounts of money.
